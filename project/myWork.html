My Work

WAIFU TACTICAL FORCE

I designed the "Waifu Tactical Force" website to introduce an adrenaline-pumping FPS game that combines anime aesthetics with tactical combat. The homepage captures the essence of intense gameplay, where players assume control of anime-style operators from elite military units. Featuring an immersive background and engaging character art, the site invites users to wishlist the game on Steam and apply for pre-alpha testing, creating excitement around the game’s release. Social media links connect players with the community, encouraging them to stay updated on new developments.

NOCTURNAL

Also created the "Nocturnal" website to highlight a gaming studio founded by former Unreal Engine 5 developers from Epic Games. The site emphasizes Nocturnal's focus on crafting unique, immersive gaming experiences that push the limits of Unreal Engine 5. With a dark, atmospheric backdrop of gothic landscapes, the homepage reflects the studio’s commitment to innovation and quality in game development, while sections like "About Us" and "Our Games" offer visitors insights into their journey and upcoming projects.
